About Us

A trusted English medium school in Makthal

Akshara High School, Makthal offers quality English medium education from Nursery to 10th Class. With teachers having 30 years of experience, strong discipline, and individual attention for every child, our school has earned the trust of parents across Makthal mandal.

Our consistent 100% pass results and the SSC District 2nd Rank & Mandal Top Rank of 2026 reflect our commitment to academic excellence. We focus on strong fundamentals, English fluency, and the all-round development of every student.
